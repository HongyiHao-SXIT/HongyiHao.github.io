A tunnel inner wall automatic correction drilling equipment

Invention Patent

A tunnel inner wall automatic correction drilling equipment, consisting of a mobile platform and a telescopic arm. The mobile platform can move along the tunnel, and the telescopic arm can rotate and swing. The drilling machine bracket at its telescopic terminal is equipped with a drilling positioning mechanism, drilling machine thruster, and drilling machine. The elastic return mechanism between the bracket and the telescopic arm terminal can straighten the bracket, and the positioning mechanism can align the drill rod radially with the inner wall of the tunnel when it contacts the inner wall.
